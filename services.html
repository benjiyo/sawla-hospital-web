Our Services

Comprehensive medical services under one roof.

Sawla General Hospital offers a wide range of inpatient and outpatient services designed to keep you and your family healthy.

🩺

General Medicine & Outpatient Clinic

Primary care for common illnesses and chronic conditions.

Our outpatient clinic is open six days a week and staffed by experienced physicians and nurses who provide consultations, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of conditions.

🚑

24/7 Emergency & Trauma Care

Immediate care when every second counts.

Our emergency department is equipped to manage accidents, injuries, acute illnesses, and other life-threatening conditions, supported by on-call specialists and a dedicated triage system.

🧬

Laboratory & Diagnostic Services

Accurate investigations for precise treatment.

We provide a full range of laboratory tests, imaging services such as X-ray and ultrasound, and cardiac diagnostics including ECG.

👶

Maternal & Child Health

Safe, supportive care for mothers and children.

Our services include antenatal and postnatal clinics, safe delivery, neonatal care, immunisation, and growth monitoring for children.

🩻

Surgical Services

Elective and emergency surgeries with modern theatre facilities.

Our surgical team performs a variety of procedures, supported by anaesthesia, sterile processing, and recovery units.
