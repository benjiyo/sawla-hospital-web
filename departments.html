Departments

Specialised units working together for you.

Our departments are designed to provide coordinated, multidisciplinary care so that every patient receives the right support at the right time.

Emergency Department

24/7 triage, resuscitation, and stabilisation of critically ill and injured patients.

Internal Medicine

Management of medical conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, and infectious diseases.

Paediatrics

Comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Maternal health, safe deliveries, gynaecological surgeries, and reproductive health services.

Surgery

General surgery, minor procedures, and post-operative care with modern theatre support.

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Lab tests, imaging, and cardiac diagnostics that support timely clinical decisions.
